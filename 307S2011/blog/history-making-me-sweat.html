History- making me sweat

Posted: Mon, 25 Apr 2011

This last class period put me under some serious historical duress. We went around the room and spoke about our specific historical topics but lo and behold, mine needs help. I am in the process of doing more research and visiting good old John Hodges and his special collections this Monday and hope I can get this hammered down so I can begin to compile sources. We read Yale’s syllabus for writing a senior thesis in history and this just stressed me further. This 40 pages is already looming over me and I am anxious about its completion. There were several essays with helpful advice in this syllabus though and they pertained to this fear I am experiencing as well as the process of choosing a topic. Several things that were stressed in this guide also include the process of editing in order to keep one on track as well as the debate between outlining or not. I tend to be the type of person who works without an outline, but for a project this large I think this would cause me to analyze in circles. I visited a man in special collections today and he seemed a little wary about my topic as well but I am going in Tuesday morning to examine more primary sources. I know this topic won’t fall out of the sky, but one can dream. Looking forward to Wednesday.

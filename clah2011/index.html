Welcome to a virtual space for the CLAH 2011 panel, Negotiating Authority: Bureaucratic and Cultural Logics of the Early Modern Spanish Empire. We've inherited the last slot on the last day of this years conference in Boston, and decided it would be nice to amplify the panel’s contributions and discussion for those who aren't able to attend the conference this year, or who are scheduled to leave before 11am on Sunday, January 9.

As we approach January, we'll be posting papers and opening paragraph-by-paragraph comments here on the site. The papers will be accessible from the table of contents over there on the right. So, join us virtually if you can’t in person!

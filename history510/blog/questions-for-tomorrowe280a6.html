Questions for tomorrow….

Posted: Sun, 24 Oct 2010

Here are a few questions to ponder before tomorrow’s class…

Q#  1: In Chapter one, as Barber and Berdan begin to define ethnography and explain it’s methodology, in what ways if at all does it differ from our definition of history or (more importantly) in its methodology?

Q# 2: What is an eitlogue? (pg 37) How does it relate to the Reality-mediation model? What are the four ways in which Mediation effects the “true” communication of stimuli? (pg 39)

Q#3: In what sense does Barber and Berdan use “deconstruction”? Given the goals of this book, is this a choice? (44)

Q#4: What are some of the issues one must consider when engaging in Paleography?

Q#5: What factors go into the creation of a calendar and how do these determine how “time” is conceived by various cultures?

Q#6: What are some of the more nuanced properties, functions, or practices in language and how does this make translation difficult? Why do you think Poetry is considered more difficult to translate than “regular” prose?

#7: What can place names tell us? (120-125) What can an individual’s name tell us about them and how can this be confusing?

# 8: What are the internal and External Methods of Textual analysis?  What relationships must an author have in relation to an event they are reporting on to be considered not only authentic but authoritative?(Chapter 7)

#9: What are the potential pitfalls of quantitative analysis? What can be gained by it?

#10: In what ways are visual and textual analyses similar/ different? When describing the uses of visual media and the pitfalls in interpretation of these media, could many of the same principals apply to both written word and pictorial art?

#11: In what ways Is the study of Maps complicated by the fact that they are representations of a reality? In what ways are these complications tied to complications in textual or visual interpretations?

#12: What are the problems with using “oral accounts” as sources? Why do Barber and Berdan conclude that textual sources are better?
